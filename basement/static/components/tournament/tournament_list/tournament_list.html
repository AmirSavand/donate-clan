Custom Tournaments

Custom tournaments on Royale Plus are not connected to Clash Royale tournaments.

Custom tournaments rules, decks, match results and winner is set by the host.

Custom tournaments are primarily made for friends or clanmates to battle and have fun.

You can not join custom tournament, you can only be invited to one.
